LION

The lion is one of the most powerful and majestic animals in the animal kingdom. It is often called the "King of the Jungle" because of its strength, courage, and dominant presence. Lions belong to the cat family and are scientifically known as Panthera leo. They are mostly found in grasslands and savannas of Africa, while a small population also lives in India. Lions are social animals and usually live in groups called prides. A pride may include several females, their cubs, and a few male lions. Male lions are easily recognized by their thick mane around the neck. They are powerful hunters and play an important role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem.
