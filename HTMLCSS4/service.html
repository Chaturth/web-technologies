Chikkamagaluru Information

Chikkamagaluru, also known as the Coffee Land of Karnataka, is a scenic hill station located in the Western Ghats. It is famous for coffee plantations, lush greenery, and trekking trails.

Popular Attractions in Chikkamagaluru

Chikkamagaluru offers a mix of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural heritage. Here are some important attractions:

1. Mullayanagiri

Mullayanagiri is the highest peak in Karnataka, popular for trekking and panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

2. Baba Budangiri

Baba Budangiri is a mountain range known for its caves, shrines, and coffee plantations. It is a spiritual and scenic destination.

3. Kudremukh National Park

Kudremukh is a biodiversity hotspot, home to wildlife, waterfalls, and trekking trails amidst rolling hills.

4. Hebbe Falls

Hebbe Falls is a stunning waterfall located inside coffee estates, offering a refreshing experience for visitors.

Top 5 Things to Do in Chikkamagaluru

  1. Trek to Mullayanagiri Peak
  2. Visit coffee plantations
  3. Explore Baba Budangiri caves
  4. Relax at Hebbe Falls
  5. Wildlife safari in Kudremukh

Specialties of Chikkamagaluru

  • World-famous coffee
  • Misty hills and valleys
  • Rich biodiversity
  • Adventure trekking
  • Historic temples and shrines
