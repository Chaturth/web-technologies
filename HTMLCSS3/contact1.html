Bengaluru Metro Information

The Bengaluru Metro, also known as Namma Metro, is a rapid transit system serving the city of Bengaluru. It provides fast, reliable, and eco-friendly transportation across the city.

Metro Lines in Bengaluru

Bengaluru Metro currently operates multiple lines connecting key parts of the city. Here are some important metro lines:

1. Purple Line

The Purple Line runs east to west, connecting Baiyappanahalli to Kengeri. It is one of the busiest lines in the network.

2. Green Line

The Green Line runs north to south, connecting Nagasandra to Silk Institute. It passes through major commercial and residential areas.

3. Yellow Line

The upcoming Yellow Line will connect R.V. Road to Bommasandra, improving connectivity to the industrial areas of Bengaluru.

4. Pink Line

The Pink Line is under construction and will connect Kalena Agrahara to Nagawara, passing through key IT hubs and residential zones.
