Bus Services in Karnataka

Karnataka has a well-connected bus network operated by KSRTC and BMTC, offering city, intercity, and luxury travel options. Here are some popular bus services in Karnataka:

1. KSRTC Airavat

Airavat is a premium bus service by KSRTC known for its comfort, air-conditioning, and long-distance travel convenience.

2. BMTC Vayu Vajra

Vayu Vajra buses connect Bengaluru city to Kempegowda International Airport, offering reliable and fast airport transfers.

3. Rajahamsa Executive

Rajahamsa buses are non-air-conditioned services by KSRTC, popular for affordable intercity travel across Karnataka.

4. Volvo City Buses

Volvo buses operated by BMTC provide comfortable city travel with air-conditioning and modern seating arrangements.
