Chikkamagaluru

Chikkamagaluru is a beautiful hill station known for coffee plantations and greenery.

Popular Attractions

1. Mullayanagiri

Highest peak in Karnataka.

2. Baba Budangiri

Famous for caves and coffee plantations.

3. Kudremukh

Known for wildlife and trekking.

4. Hebbe Falls

Beautiful waterfall inside estates.
